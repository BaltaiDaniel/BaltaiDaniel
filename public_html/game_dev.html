How it All Started
.......................................

Growing up as a child, I loved playing video games a lot. I remember downloading over 20 different video games on my aunt's Nokia C2, all under 10MB :D GTA was the reigning video game in middle school, and pretty much every guy in my class who had a laptop, had GTA on his laptop.
My Game Development journey started during the lockdown in 2020. I started with the Godot Game Engine. My first project was an attempt to clone Call of Duty. I never start a new thing from the basics. Of course, till this day I haven't finished that project, but it doesn't mean I've given up. I don't give up and I don't quit.
