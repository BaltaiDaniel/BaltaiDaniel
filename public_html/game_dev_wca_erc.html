About WCA


Word Chase Adventure is an educational video game that merges the traditional platformer genre with educational content. Developed over a period of more than six months, Word Chase Adventure endeavors to enhance the learning journey for its players.
With an audience in rural Nigeria, the game experience begins from the base level of recognizing letters up to the point of forming simple sentences.
